About Us

SEKUNTUM

SEKUNTUM

The name Sekuntum reminds us of Sharifah Rose. Her strong and authentic personality. How she always chooses to bloom. Like a bunch of beautiful, fresh flowers. No matter what she goes through, she always chooses to rise up. Because when there's no rain, there's no flowers. She's not afraid to be her real self in a room full of people. She's proud of her journey, her success and most importantly her self. And that is what Sekuntum is about. Self-love, self-growth and self-confidence. Sekuntum is here to remind you — everyone's blooming in our own way. We're all different but we're all here trying. We're all here with different stories and journeys. At the end of the day, all that matters is that we're proud of being our own true selves.
